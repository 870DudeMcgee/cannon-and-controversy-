# Episode 08 Prompt Packs

Episode 08 focuses on the books themselves: the seven deuterocanonical books, the Greek additions to Esther and Daniel, and the claim that these texts are not lost but still present in Catholic and Orthodox Bibles.

Use these packs to populate:
- opener and chapter-title variants
- book-profile cards for Tobit, Judith, 1 and 2 Maccabees, Wisdom, Sirach, and Baruch
- comparison cards for the Greek additions to Esther and Daniel
- end-cards for the claim that these books were excluded, not erased

This episode should feel like a guided inventory episode. The governing argument is that the removed books are concrete texts with specific themes, historical roles, and theological consequences, not a vague missing category.

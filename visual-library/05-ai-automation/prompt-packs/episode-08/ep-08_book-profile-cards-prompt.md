# Episode 08 Book Profile Cards Prompt Pack

Generate concise documentary-series text in a scholarly but cinematic tone.

Task goal:
Generate book-profile cards, evidence strips, comparison-card variants, and end-card lines for Episode 08.

Target use:
book cards, evidence strips, lower thirds, comparison cards, end-cards

Text inputs:
- Tobit:
  - angels with proper names
  - demon Asmodeus
  - prayer and almsgiving
- Judith:
  - female heroism
  - faith under siege
  - theological historical-novel mode
- 1 Maccabees:
  - historical chronicle
  - abomination of desolation context
  - Hanukkah origin
- 2 Maccabees:
  - martyrdom
  - resurrection
  - prayers for the dead
- Wisdom of Solomon:
  - immortality of the soul
  - divine Wisdom
  - critique of idolatry
- Sirach:
  - practical wisdom
  - James parallels
  - Hebrew original recovered
- Baruch:
  - repentance
  - exile
  - attached to Jeremiah in ancient manuscript tradition
- additions to existing books:
  - Esther Greek additions
  - Song of the Three Young Men
  - Susanna
  - Bel and the Dragon
- framing lines:
  - these books are not lost
  - they are still printed in Catholic Bibles today
  - they were specifically excluded from Protestant Bibles beginning with Luther in 1534

Style rules:
- concise, sober, and confident
- readable on screen
- suitable for inventory cards and profile strips
- emphasize what each book contributes rather than only that it was removed

Generate:
- 7 book-profile card variants or headings, one for each main book
- 4 evidence-strip sets for the additions to Esther and Daniel
- 4 short end-card lines for the excluded-not-erased conclusion
- 4 lower-third sets covering the key book identifiers and themes

Must avoid:
- sensationalism
- reducing every book to one doctrine fight only
- excessive jargon
- excessive length

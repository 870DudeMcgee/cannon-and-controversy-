# Episode 07 Prompt Packs

Episode 07 focuses on stakes and consequences: what changed when the books were removed, why the canon debate is not merely academic, and how doctrine, history, and New Testament readability are altered by source loss.

Use these packs to populate:
- opener and chapter-title variants
- consequence cards for doctrine, cosmology, history, and literary context
- quote cards and evidence strips for Wisdom 3, 2 Maccabees, 1 Enoch, and the intertestamental history problem
- end-cards for the claim that canon decisions changed what billions of people could read directly in scripture

This episode should feel like a culmination episode. The governing argument is that canon decisions changed doctrine outcomes, historical memory, and the reader's access to the New Testament's own source world.

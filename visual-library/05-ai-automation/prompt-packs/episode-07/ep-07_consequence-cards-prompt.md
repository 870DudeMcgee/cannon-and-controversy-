# Episode 07 Consequence Cards Prompt Pack

Generate concise documentary-series text in a scholarly but cinematic tone.

Task goal:
Generate consequence cards, quote-card copy, evidence strips, and end-card variants for Episode 07.

Target use:
comparison cards, quote cards, evidence strips, lower thirds, end-cards

Text inputs:
- consequence categories:
  - purgatory and prayers for the dead
  - immortality of the soul
  - cosmic origin of evil and the Watchers
  - historical context of the New Testament
  - literary foundation of the New Testament
- key texts and anchors:
  - 2 Maccabees 12:44-46
  - Wisdom 3:1-4
  - 1 Enoch and the Watchers story
  - 1 and 2 Maccabees as the intertestamental bridge
  - John 10:22 as Hanukkah historical continuity
- framing lines:
  - these decisions shaped what billions believe
  - to read the New Testament without these books is to read a letter without knowing the conversation it was part of
  - the Bible was not handed down intact from heaven
  - history is not a threat to faith; it is a richer story

Style rules:
- concise, sober, and confident
- readable on screen
- suitable for consequence cards and evidence strips
- emphasize stakes and continuity rather than outrage

Generate:
- 4 consequence-card variants for doctrine and afterlife stakes
- 4 evidence-strip sets for Watchers, Maccabees, and New Testament context
- 4 quote-card variants for Wisdom 3 and the final perspective note
- 4 short end-card lines for the richer-story conclusion

Must avoid:
- sensationalism
- overclaiming that one consequence settles every theological dispute
- excessive jargon
- excessive length

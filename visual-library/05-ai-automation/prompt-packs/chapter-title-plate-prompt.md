- aged gold rulework
- restrained deep red accents
- codex and manuscript tactility
- scholarly, cinematic, tactile composition

Support these motifs if relevant:

- illuminated-borders
- glowing-parchment-reveal

- aged gold title emphasis
- parchment or dark manuscript field
- border or corner detail
- clear title hierarchy

Must avoid:
- movie trailer bombast
- chrome metallic gold
- modern sans-serif poster look
- busy texture everywhere
- neon highlights

Output requirements:

- orientation: landscape
- deliver 4 variations
- keep the composition clean and legible
- avoid modern graphic-design clichés

If text appears in the image, keep it minimal, aged, and readable.

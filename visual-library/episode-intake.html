Episode Bridge

Move approved work into episode buckets.

This page is the bridge from reusable library work to an actual episode package. Use it after a source item, generated asset, or text variant has already survived review.

The goal is not to rebuild the whole archive per episode. The goal is to create a focused intake layer with clear shot buckets and clean provenance.

What To Move In

Approved Generated Work

  • map studies that passed review
  • usable chapter-title plates
  • approved quote or lower-third text variants

Approved Sourced Material

  • curated manuscripts
  • curated maps
  • curated paintings or ruins that serve one narrative beat

Keep Out

  • raw prompt experiments
  • uncertain historical material
  • variants without run logs or provenance

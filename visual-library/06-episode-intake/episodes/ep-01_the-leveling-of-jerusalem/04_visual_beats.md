# 04 Visual Beats: Episode 01

- episode title: The Leveling of Jerusalem
- episode code: ep-01_the-leveling-of-jerusalem
- use with: `02_production.md`, `05_assets_text.md`, and `06_shot_bucket.csv`
- purpose: first assembly guide for narration beats, screen text, and visual order

## Editorial Rule

Every beat should push the viewer from political execution, to widening unrest, to Jerusalem's collapse, to canon consequences. Do not park too long on devotional imagery or ruin spectacle.

### Beat 1

- visual:
  - cold open parchment reveal into the title plate
- narration beat:
  - Open on the claim that the crucifixion was not the end of a story but the ignition of a forty-year crisis.
- screen text:
  - The Forty-Year Fuse
- notes:
  - Hold only long enough to establish tone and title.

### Beat 2

- visual:
  - immediate locator map locked on Jerusalem
- narration beat:
  - Anchor the episode in Jerusalem before widening to empire.

  - Jerusalem
- notes:

## Assembly Notes

- Keep Beat 1 through Beat 8 under tight discipline; if the opener drifts into abstraction, the whole episode loses its frame.
- Beat 11 through Beat 15 should widen the crisis without feeling like a travel montage.
- Beat 17 through Beat 20 is the emotional and structural hinge of the episode.
- Beat 21 through Beat 28 must move fast enough to feel inevitable.
- In Beat 21 through Beat 28, simplify the visuals as the narration intensifies; slow pans, ash/ruin atmosphere, and clean text will read better than complex tables or busy maps.

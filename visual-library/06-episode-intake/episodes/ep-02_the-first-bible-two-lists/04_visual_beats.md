- episode title: The First Bible: Two Different Lists
- episode code: ep-02_the-first-bible-two-lists
- use with: `02_production.md`, `05_assets_text.md`, and `06_shot_bucket.csv`

## Editorial Rule

Every beat should push the viewer from canon as a human decision, to the two scriptural worlds, to early Christian inheritance, to the `removed, not added` conclusion. Do not let the Reformation arrive before the older divergence is clear.

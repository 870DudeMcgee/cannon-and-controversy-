## AI Prompt Pack

### 01. Excluded Not Lost Correction Plate

- deliverable: `ep-08_excluded-not-lost-correction_v01.png`
- purpose: support the opening correction that these books were excluded, not lost
- prompt:
  - Quiet documentary opener plate showing preserved ancient books on a shelf or table in active Christian manuscript tradition, codex and parchment textures, no sense of disappearance or ruin, historically grounded archive atmosphere, broad center negative space for correction text, restrained palette, no baked text

### 02. Seven-Book Profile Set

- deliverable: `ep-08_seven-book-profile-set_v01.png`
- purpose: provide one memorable visual identity for each removed book without turning the episode into fantasy illustration
- prompt:
  - Create a matched set of 7 documentary profile plates for Tobit, Judith, 1 Maccabees, 2 Maccabees, Wisdom of Solomon, Sirach, and Baruch. Each plate should have one strong identifying historical-literary mood, ancient manuscript or codex context, restrained symbolic detail, and broad clean negative space for lower thirds and profile text. Keep the set visually unified but distinct by book. No baked text, no fantasy character posters, no modern costumes.
- variants:
  - Tobit: travel, prayer, angelic guidance, domestic piety
  - Judith: siege, courage, deliverance under pressure
  - 1 Maccabees: revolt, history, Temple crisis, political chronicle
  - 2 Maccabees: martyrdom, resurrection hope, prayer for the dead
  - Wisdom of Solomon: philosophical theology, divine wisdom, Alexandria mood
  - Sirach: practical wisdom, teaching, Jerusalem classroom atmosphere
  - Baruch: exile, repentance, Jeremiah-linked textual memory

### 03. Wisdom And Exile Support Plate

- deliverable: `ep-08_wisdom-exile-support_v01.png`

- prompt:

### 04. Esther Additions Comparison Plate

### 06. Excluded Not Erased Closing Plate

# Episode 08 README

Episode 08 covers the removed books themselves: the seven deuterocanonical books plus the Greek additions to Esther and Daniel.

Use this folder as the working package for:
- episode framing and approved inputs in `01_package.md`
- production structure and first-cut rules in `02_production.md`
- spoken draft in `03_script.md`
- first assembly order in `04_visual_beats.md`
- on-screen text in `05_assets_text.md`
- shot inventory in `06_shot_bucket.csv`

Narrative role in the series:
- follows Episode 07's stakes and consequence arc
- turns to the concrete identity of the books themselves
- prepares the series to finish with the canon timeline in Episode 09

Episode code:
- `ep-08_the-removed-books-what-they-are`

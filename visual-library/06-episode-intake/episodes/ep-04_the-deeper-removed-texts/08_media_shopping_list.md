## AI Prompt Pack

### 01. Qumran Context Plate

- deliverable: `ep-04_qumran-context-plate_v01.png`
- purpose: orient the viewer in the Judean desert and Dead Sea world before the cave evidence tightens
- prompt:
  - Archaeological documentary plate of Qumran in the Judean desert near the Dead Sea, dry pale terrain, low settlement remains, cave-bearing cliffs in the distance, sober excavation mood, historical and material rather than mystical, muted stone dust parchment palette, room for chapter card and locator overlays, no tourists, no baked text
- avoid:
  - fantasy desert monastery look, treasure-hunt adventure poster, dramatic glowing cave mouth

### 02. Cave Preservation Contrast Set

- deliverable: `ep-04_cave-preservation-contrast-set_v01.png`
- purpose: show Cave 4 versus Caves 1 and 11 as a readable material contrast
- prompt:
  - Create a matched set of 2 documentary archaeological plates contrasting Dead Sea Scroll cave deposits: one plate shows a chaotic fragment-rich storage environment like Cave 4, one plate shows a more deliberate jar-preserved concealment environment like Caves 1 or 11, restrained desert stone palette, fragment and jar logic clear, no fantasy relic glow, safe negative space for evidence overlays, no baked text

### 03. Scroll Fragment Archive Plate

- deliverable: `ep-04_scroll-fragment-archive_v01.png`
- purpose: support the transition from site evidence to what the fragments prove
- prompt:
  - Close documentary archive plate of Dead Sea Scroll fragments, parchment pieces, careful arrangement, scholarly preservation context, subdued lighting, material evidence centered, visually legible texture, no museum glare, no mystical aura, clean side space for quote or evidence card overlay, no baked text

### 04. Disaster-Recovery Plate

- deliverable: `ep-04_disaster-recovery-plate_v01.png`
- purpose: support the line that Rome destroyed the node but the data survived
- prompt:
  - Conceptual but historically grounded documentary plate showing emergency preservation of scrolls before catastrophe, jars, wrapped manuscripts, concealment in a cave environment, restrained urgency, no action hero staging, archaeological realism, ash and dust atmosphere, room for quote overlay, no baked text
- avoid:
  - sci-fi data metaphors, glowing relic imagery, cinematic chase scene

### 05. 1 Enoch Preservation Plate

- deliverable: `ep-04_1enoch-preservation-plate_v01.png`
- purpose: anchor the Enoch sequence as ancient, copied, and preserved before later exclusion
- prompt:
  - Documentary manuscript plate centered on ancient Enochic text preservation, Aramaic-fragment feel, careful scholarly presentation, austere reverence without mysticism, parchment fibers, deep historical time, negative space for `1 Enoch preserved` style overlays, no fake readable modern text, no baked text

### 06. Ethiopian Continuity Plate

- deliverable: `ep-04_ethiopian-continuity-plate_v01.png`
- purpose: support the continued life of Enoch in the Ethiopian Orthodox tradition
- prompt:
  - Historical Ethiopian Christian manuscript continuity plate, illuminated manuscript tradition, dignified ecclesial setting, warm parchment and dark wood palette, visually distinct from Qumran desert imagery while still documentary and restrained, room for lower third or evidence strip, no exoticizing fantasy treatment, no baked text

- episode code: ep-04_the-deeper-removed-texts
- use with: `02_production.md`, `05_assets_text.md`, and `06_shot_bucket.csv`

## Editorial Rule

Every beat should push the viewer from the official-list problem, to Qumran under pressure, to what the Scrolls prove, to 1 Enoch as the clearest case. Keep the episode archaeological and evidentiary, never mystical.

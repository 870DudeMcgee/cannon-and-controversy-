## Notes

This episode should feel like an archaeological reveal with historical consequences. The narrative job is to show that the canon story is not only about official lists; it is also about which textual worlds survived crisis, were copied heavily, and were later left outside formal Western canons.

Use `04_visual_beats.md` as the working order for the first assembly, `03_script.md` as the spoken rehearsal script, and `05_assets_text.md` as the default first-cut sheet for identifiers, thesis cards, bridge cards, and evidence.

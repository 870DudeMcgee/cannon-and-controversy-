# Sixth Pack Manifest

This pack focuses on chapter-intro animation plates and council/decree title systems.

- `chapter-intro-plate-01.svg`
  - widescreen ceremonial chapter opener for institutional turning points
- `chapter-intro-plate-02.svg`
  - vertical decree card for shorts and portrait cutdowns
- `council-title-card-01.svg`
  - landscape council title card for canon-definition and policy chapters

## Supporting Elements

- `decree-ribbon-01.svg`
  - decree banner for session labels and formal headings
- `canon-seal-badge-01.svg`
  - seal-like date badge for canon or decree emphasis

## Recommended Uses

1. Pair `chapter-intro-plate-01.svg` with `dust-field-01.svg` and `glow-veil-01.svg`.
2. Use `decree-ribbon-01.svg` as a top-layer label over chapter cards and map sequences.
3. Use `canon-seal-badge-01.svg` when a date or decree needs ceremonial authority.

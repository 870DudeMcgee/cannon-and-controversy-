# Scroll Textures

## Collect First

- torn parchment
- curled edges
- crease shadows
- wax stains
- soot and edge burn details
- subtle grain and dust
- rolled scroll silhouettes

## Strong Search Phrases

- parchment texture scan
- torn vellum edge texture
- old paper crease texture
- burned parchment edge
- manuscript stain texture

## Promote To Curated If

- the texture loops or tiles well
- the edges can isolate cleanly for overlays
- the asset grades well into the site palette
- it can support at least two transition types

## Motif Links

- glowing parchment reveal
- codex-opening transition
- illuminated borders
- red/gold text reveals

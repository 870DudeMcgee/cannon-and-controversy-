# Source Hit List

Use this as the first-pass hunting guide for filling the library fast.

## Public-Domain First

Prioritize these institutions before paid stock:

- The Met Open Access
- British Library digitized manuscripts
- Library of Congress digital collections
- New York Public Library digital collections
- Europeana
- Rijksmuseum open collection
- National Gallery of Art open access
- Wikimedia Commons
- Rawpixel public domain
- Openverse

## Paid Support When Needed

Use paid sources only for assets that are hard to get in open collections:

- moving candle plates
- cinematic dust and particles
- dramatic church atmosphere shots
- highly controlled map textures
- macro parchment motion plates

## Fast Triage Rule

For every candidate asset, ask:

- Does it look like it belongs in the same world as the site?
- Can it serve one of the six signature motifs?
- Can it be reused in at least two episodes?
- Is the license clear enough to keep?

If the answer is not mostly yes, do not promote it to `curated/`.

# Asset Sourcing Plan

## Target Outcome

Build a series-wide image and motion library that can support documentary intros, chapter opens, map sequences, timelines, scripture callouts, historical reconstructions, and reflective transitions.

## Collection Targets

Aim for these minimum curated totals before heavy episode assembly:

- Manuscripts: 200
- Roman Empire: 150
- Temple/Jerusalem: 150
- Maps: 120
- Scroll textures: 80
- Paintings: 120
- Candles: 60
- Ethiopian art: 120
- Church interiors: 100
- Ancient ruins: 100
- Typography references: 80

## Priority Order

1. Signature motifs and reusable overlays
2. Manuscripts, maps, and Temple/Jerusalem references
3. Ethiopian art and typography references
4. Roman Empire, ruins, paintings, and church interiors
5. Candles and atmospheric inserts

## Category Briefs

### Manuscripts

Collect:
- Gospel folios
- marginal ornament
- rubrication
- illuminated initials
- vellum texture
- codex pages at different angles
- close macro details of ink, cracking, and gold

Good source types:
- museum and library digitization portals
- public domain manuscript repositories
- scans from archive books and facsimiles

Reusable outputs:
- parchment reveal backgrounds
- scripture quote beds
- codex-opening inserts
- decorative corners and borders

### Roman Empire

Collect:
- busts of emperors
- coins
- military reliefs
- inscriptions
- marble texture
- Roman roads and architecture
- mosaics and standards

Reusable outputs:
- imperial chapter intros
- map overlays
- timeline markers for decrees, councils, and conquests

## Source Pools To Mine

Start with these before paying for assets:

- The Met Open Access
- British Library digitized manuscripts
- Library of Congress digital collections
- Wikimedia Commons for public-domain historical works
- Europeana collections
- New York Public Library digital collections
- Rijksmuseum open collections
- National Gallery of Art open access
- Rawpixel public domain
- Openverse

Use paid libraries only where atmosphere or motion plates are missing:

- Artgrid
- Shutterstock
- Adobe Stock
- Envato
- Filmsupply
- Motion Array
- Pexels for limited atmosphere support after license review

## Ingest Workflow

1. Drop everything into `03-ingest/inbox/`.
2. Move uncertain rights into `03-ingest/to-license-check/`.
3. Rename and tag approved assets.
4. Move approved items into category `raw/`.
5. Promote only the strongest assets into `curated/`.
6. Derive masks, overlays, and crops into `cutouts/` and `overlays/`.
7. Record every approved asset in `asset-tracker.csv`.
